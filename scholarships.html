🎓 Scholarships

🎓 National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Central Government scholarship platform for students.

Eligibility

  • Indian citizen
  • Student studying in a recognized institution
  • Must satisfy income criteria of the scholarship

Benefits

  • Financial support for education
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
  • Supports school and college students

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Educational Certificates

Application Process

  1. Register on NSP Portal
  2. Fill scholarship application
  3. Upload documents
  4. Submit for verification

🏆 PM YASASVI Scholarship

Scholarship support for deserving students from OBC, EBC and DNT categories.

Eligibility

  • Students belonging to eligible categories
  • Studying in Class 9 or Class 11
  • Family income within prescribed limits

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for studies
  • Support for academic growth
  • Encourages higher education

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • School ID Card

Application Process

  1. Check eligibility
  2. Complete online registration
  3. Upload required documents
  4. Submit application

📚 Central Sector Scholarship

Financial assistance for meritorious students pursuing higher education.

Eligibility

  • Students with excellent academic performance
  • Enrolled in recognized colleges or universities
  • Family income within prescribed limits

Benefits

  • Annual scholarship support
  • Helps cover education expenses
  • Encourages higher studies

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Marksheet
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details

Application Process

  1. Register on scholarship portal
  2. Fill application form
  3. Upload documents
  4. Submit application

🎓 Post Matric Scholarship

Financial support for students continuing education after matriculation.

Eligibility

  • Students studying after Class 10
  • Belonging to eligible categories
  • Meeting income criteria

Benefits

  • Scholarship for tuition fees
  • Maintenance allowance
  • Support for higher education

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Educational Records

Application Process

  1. Complete registration
  2. Fill scholarship form
  3. Upload documents
  4. Submit for approval
