🏛 Government Schemes

🎓 PM Vidyalaxmi Scheme

Education loan support for students pursuing higher education.

Eligibility

  • Students pursuing higher education
  • Admission in a recognized institution

Benefits

  • Education loan assistance
  • Financial support for studies

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Admission Letter

Application Process

  1. Register online
  2. Upload documents
  3. Submit application

💰 PM Mudra Loan

Financial assistance for small businesses and startups.

Eligibility

  • Small business owners
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Startups

Benefits

  • Business loan up to eligible limits
  • No collateral for certain categories

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Business Details

Application Process

  1. Choose a bank
  2. Submit application
  3. Verification and approval

🏥 Ayushman Bharat

Health insurance coverage for eligible families.

Eligibility

  • Economically weaker families
  • Eligible beneficiaries under government criteria
  • Families listed in SECC database

Benefits

  • Cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals
  • Health coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family
  • Coverage for major medical procedures

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Mobile Number
  • Family Identification Documents

Application Process

  1. Check eligibility online
  2. Visit nearest CSC center
  3. Verify documents
  4. Receive Ayushman Card

🏠 PM Awas Yojana

Affordable housing assistance for eligible citizens.

Eligibility

  • Economically Weaker Section (EWS)
  • Low Income Group (LIG)
  • Middle Income Group (MIG)
  • Applicant should not own a pucca house

Benefits

  • Housing subsidy from the government
  • Reduced home loan interest burden
  • Financial support for house construction

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Address Proof
  • Bank Account Details

Application Process

  1. Register on the PMAY portal
  2. Fill application form
  3. Upload required documents
  4. Submit application for verification
